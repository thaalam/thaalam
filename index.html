Who we are

Thaalam is a brand committed to making a difference in the lives of individuals with hearing impairments. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to experience the joy and power of music. Our mission is to create high-quality, accessible musical instruments specifically designed for the hearing impaired community. Through our innovative technology and expert craftsmanship, we aim to empower individuals to express themselves and connect with the world of music in a whole new way

About Us

Vision

To empower and inspire the hearing impaired community through the manufacturing innovative musical instruments, enabling them to express themselves and connect with the world of music like never before.

At Thaalam, our mission is to make music more accessible for everyone. We design and manufacture pianos that are helpful for people with hearing impairments and those who want to learn music. We aim to work with schools and other organisations to promote music education and make our products more affordable for everyone.
